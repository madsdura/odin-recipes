Apple Hors d'Oeuvres

Very festive looking. Great for a summer dinner or cocktail party. Must be served immediately for best results. -Eve Gronberg

Assembly Time: 10 minutes per apple

Equipment Needed: sharp knife, butter knife

Ingredients:

  • Granny Smith Apples
  • Boursin Cheese
  • Anise

Process:

  1. Slice apples into half or quarter circle pieces, leaving skin on (this adds to the flavor) approximately 1/4-inch thick.
  2. Spread Boursin cheese on top of each piece.
  3. Place a small Anise leaf on the top of each piece.
  4. Serve.
