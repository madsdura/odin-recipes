Brownies

If you like chocolate, this is extra-special. -Cheryl W. Kelly

Yield: 16 pieces

Assembly Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Equipment Needed: bowl, spoon, 9"x13" pan

Ingredients:

  • 3 1-oz squares unsweetend chocolate
  • 1/2 cup margarine or butter
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 eggs

Process:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  3. Melt margarine or butter with chocolate. Add to bowl with dry ingredients.
  4. Add vanilla and eggs and mix everything well by hand.
  5. Pat mixture into greased 9"x13" pan.
  6. Bake at 350° for 20 minutes.
